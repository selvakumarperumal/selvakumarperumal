### 📖 The short story

> Most AI demos look brilliant in a notebook and fall apart the moment real traffic shows up.
> That gap — between `it_works_on_my_machine()` and `it_survives_2am_production_traffic()` — is where I live.

I build voice agents that reason before they speak, multi-agent systems that actually coordinate, and RAG pipelines that retrieve facts instead of inventing them — then I take all of it past the demo, onto Kubernetes, behind autoscalers, wired into monitoring that tells the truth.
